Related Projects
================

Space ROS makes use of several external projects that aren't part of standard ROS 2 distributions.
IKOS and Cobra are static analysis tools that have been integrated into Space ROS using Python-based wrappers (ament_cobra and ament_ikos), similar to how other analyzers are integrated into ROS 2.
Doorstop and FRET are requirements tools that are part of an :doc:`overall requirements methodology <Tutorials/Requirements-Tools-And-Processes>`.
